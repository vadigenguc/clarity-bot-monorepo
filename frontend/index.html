Your Questions, Answered.

How does Clarity Bot solve information overload?+

Clarity Bot acts as your project's single source of truth. It securely listens to conversations, reads documents, and transcribes meetings to build an intelligent knowledge base. Instead of searching, you can simply ask and get an instant, accurate answer with sources, saving hours of work.

How does Clarity Bot fix the broken workflow between Slack and Jira?+

Clarity Bot eliminates context-switching. It identifies tasks in Slack conversations and automatically drafts Jira tickets pre-filled with all relevant context. You can approve or edit them directly in Slack, boosting your team's task creation workflow by up to 80%.

How does Clarity Bot help with team alignment?+

Clarity Bot creates a central brain for your project, ensuring everyone is on the same page, regardless of their time zone. It provides consistent, accurate answers from the project's knowledge base, reducing the need for constant check-in meetings.

What makes Clarity Bot's AI different from a standard chatbot?+

Clarity Bot is a specialist AI assistant that learns the unique context of your project. It uses a powerful technique called Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), which means it doesn't guess. It first retrieves the actual facts from your project's documents, conversations, and meeting transcripts, and then generates an answer based only on that verified information. This prevents the "hallucinations" or incorrect answers common with general AI and ensures all information is grounded in your project's reality.

How does Clarity Bot handle meetings and video calls?+

You can upload any audio or video recording. Clarity Bot will transcribe it with high accuracy, generate a concise summary of key decisions and action items, and make the entire transcript searchable, so you can ask questions about it later.

How does the automated Jira ticket creation work?+

It's a three-step process: 1) Clarity Bot's AI identifies a task in a conversation. 2) It drafts a Jira ticket with suggested fields and sends it to you privately in Slack. 3) You can approve, edit, or discard the ticket with a single click.

What tools and file types does Clarity Bot support?+

Clarity Bot's deepest integration at launch is with Jira, with active development for Confluence, Asana, Trello, and GitHub. It supports PDF, DOCX, TXT, XLSX, and CSV files, and can transcribe MP3 and MP4 audio/video files.
