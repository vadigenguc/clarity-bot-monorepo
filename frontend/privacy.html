2. How We Use Information

  1. To provide and improve the Service.
  2. To generate AI-powered responses, summaries, and task automation based on project data.

3. Data Handling and Security

By the nature of how AI systems work, certain user data may be processed by large language models (LLMs) to deliver the Service. We ensure that all data is securely transferred and stored end-to-end, with strict access controls, and we do not share your data with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Data is only shared with third-party providers as necessary to operate the Service (for example, payment processors or secure cloud hosting services). All providers are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

5. Cookie Policy

We use cookies only in connection with Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage patterns. These cookies help us improve the Service experience. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking beyond analytics, and we do not collect any other cookie data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some analytics features may be limited.
