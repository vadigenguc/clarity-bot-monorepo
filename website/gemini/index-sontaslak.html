Frequently Asked Questions

Is my project and conversation data secure? +

Yes, security is our top priority. Clarity is built with enterprise-grade security standards. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Your project's knowledge base is completely isolated to your workspace using Supabase's Row Level Security, meaning no other workspace can ever access your data. We are committed to being fully compliant with Slack's security reviews for marketplace apps.

How does Clarity actually understand our project? +

Clarity builds its knowledge base from the content you allow it to see. It uses advanced **NLP (Natural Language Processing) and ML (Machine Learning) engines** to process text from conversations and documents. This text is converted into a searchable "vector" format that captures the underlying meaning and context, allowing Clarity to understand concepts, not just keywords.

What makes Clarity's AI different from a standard chatbot? +

While standard chatbots have general knowledge, Clarity is a specialist. It uses a technique called Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). This means it doesn't just guess the answer; it first *retrieves* the actual facts from your specific project documents and conversations, and then uses that information to generate a precise, context-aware answer. This prevents hallucinations and ensures the information is grounded in your project's reality.

How is this different from other Slack to Jira integrations? +

Most integrations are simple command-line tools. Clarity acts as an intelligent **orchestrator agent**. It doesn't just pass commands; it understands the *context* behind them. It learns from your documents and conversations to suggest projects, populate descriptions, and answer follow-up questions, acting as a true AI project assistant rather than just a form-filler.

Can this help our agile team in Istanbul? +

Absolutely. Clarity is designed for the high-speed, often asynchronous, nature of modern tech teams, whether they're in Istanbul, London, or working remotely. By creating a central brain for your project, it ensures that team members across different time zones are always aligned. It reduces the need for constant check-in meetings and allows developers, designers, and managers to get the information they need, when they need it, directly in Slack.

What integrations will be supported? +

Our primary and deepest integration at launch is with Jira, as it's the core of many project management workflows. We are actively developing integrations for other popular tools like Confluence, GitHub, and Figma based on user feedback from our waitlist. Our goal is to connect all the places your project knowledge lives.

How will pricing work? +

We will offer several subscription tiers, including a free plan for small teams to get started. Paid plans will be based on factors like the number of users, the volume of data processed (e.g., meeting transcriptions), and access to advanced features. Full pricing details will be released closer to launch. Join the waitlist for early-bird offers!
